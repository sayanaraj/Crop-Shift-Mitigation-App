🏛️ Active Government Schemes

Subsidies & support programs available for oilseed farmers

🌱
PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi
Direct income support of ₹6,000/year in three equal installments to all landholding farmer families.
✓ You may be eligible · Apply at pmkisan.gov.in
🫘
NFSM — Oilseeds Programme
National Food Security Mission provides seed kits, demonstrations, and training for oilseed crop expansion.
✓ Seed subsidy up to 50% · Contact district agriculture office
🌴
National Mission on Edible Oils – Oil Palm
₹29,000/ha viability gap payment + planting material subsidy for oil palm cultivation under NMEO-OP.
✓ High subsidy · Suitable for Andhra, Telangana, Northeast
🌻
State Oilseed Mission
State-level subsidies on certified seeds, micro-irrigation, and farm machinery for sunflower & groundnut cultivation.
✓ Varies by state · Check state agriculture portal
🛡️
Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
Crop insurance at just 1.5% (Rabi) and 2% (Kharif) premium. Covers losses from natural calamities, pests, and disease.
✓ Enroll by season deadline · pmfby.gov.in
