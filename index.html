NMEO-OS

National Mission on Edible Oils – Oilseeds

Benefits: ₹10,000/ha subsidy + seed kits
Eligibility: Farmers with ≥1 acre oilseed land
Profit Impact: +₹2,400/acre

PM-KISAN

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi

Benefits: ₹6,000/year direct transfer
Eligibility: All small & marginal farmers
Profit Impact: +₹6,000/year
